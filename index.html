ABOUT ITIHA

Itiha, The national Techno-Cultural Fest of Rajadhani Institute of Engineering & Technology is the collective labor of every member of the RIET family and has always been a source of great enthusiasm and cooperation, a manifestation of promising ideas and talents of students. It is a sublime amalgamation of technical and cultural events. Each year the events get a step ahead and emerge as a great success and Itiha 2023 is not going to be an exception.

RIET

Rajadhani Institute of Engineering & Technology stands for grooming the Youth as Innovative, Creative, and Empathetic Technologists, Managers, and Entrepreneurs for Social Transformation. Our mission is to encourage the holistic development of students with well-balanced and student-centric curricular, co-curricular, and extracurricular activities. We, Rajadhani Institute Of Engineering & Technology were successful in promoting the technical as well as the entrepreneurial mindset in the students such as Travancore Majestic, CE Square, Karma Research Lab, etc.. do the same for this.
